Payment Policy Security For Travmaks

Your security is our responsibility, TRAVMAKS ensures that every transaction you conduct online is in a safe and secure environment. To achieve this, TRAVMAKS is protected by Secured Socket Layer (SSL), the leaders in encryption technology. The process of encryption takes your transaction details and converts it into bits of code that are securely transmitted over the Internet. This scrambled data cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. Your transaction details may be shared with our service providers or vendors to the extent deemed to be necessary. Paytm’s triple handshaking technique ensure the total security in every transaction. For more security related information you can visit paytm policy page.

If you have any additional queries or concerns please email us and we will reply within 24 hours.


CANCELLATION CHARGES

If the Outbound Tour is cancelled for any reason what-so-ever, including cases of visa rejection or voluntary cancellation by the User the cancellation charges as mentioned in the brochure or the itinerary will apply. In the absence of any such information, the following cancellation charges will apply:


Time period in which the cancellation is made Charges
45 days or more, prior to departure Booking fees
Between 44 and 31 days of departure 25% of Tour Cost
Between 30 and 15 days of departure 50% of Tour Cost
Between 14 and 8 days of departure 75% of Tour Cost
Within 7days prior to date of departure, or no-show for any reason what-so-ever 100% of Tour Cost
